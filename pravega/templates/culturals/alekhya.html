Description

Art is where imagination meets reality, where a soul expresses its emotions connecting fantasy and practicality. A playground for the thoughtful ideas of an artist, art, can transcend the barrier of human individuality and its essence and ethos inspire the combined spirit of humanity.

Painting and designing are an integral part of art. Its clutches capture the attention of the audience and creator alike, defying the norms ruling the segregation of space and time. Here, in Alekhya, we give you a canvas, an opportunity to exhibit the artist in you, through a unique art competition, where you get to portray ideas through the colours of your soul. So, do not dally…let your colours speak of your heart and become a part of this epic celebration.
