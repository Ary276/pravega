Memecraft

Knock Knock. Who's there?

It’s us, the meme-lords of IISc, Bangalore. We are here with Memecraft: a Pravega 2020 Contest where you can craft your own rib-tickling memes to become memortal in our century-old meme heritage and win exciting cash prizes as well!

Remember all those times scrolling through memes on Reddit or Instagram, cackling with unstoppable laughter, rolling over your couch onto the floor holding your stomach so that it won’t implode? Well, here’s your chance to create your own stomach-imploding memes about anything or everything; just make sure you do not upset the Queen of England!

Since this is a contest, of course there will be winners (who may not have time for losers-pun intended) but remember, this event is not about winning or losing, but rather about showing your creativity, wit, and humor. To aid this, we will be posting your entries on our official Instagram handle (insert hyperlink), along with your names. The number of likes you receive will contribute as 50 % of our judging criteria. The other 50 % will be upto our panel of judges (we will make sure they have a good sense of humor!).

Tip: You may want to share the post on our page with your entry to popularize it and get as many likes as possible.

So, give us your wittiest memes and Happy Memecrafting!


Rules and Instructions

  1. Each participant can submit one, and only one, entry in the contest. All submissions are to be made by sending an email to meme.event.pravega@gmail.com. The e-mail should be sent from your contact e-mail ID and should contain your Name (as you want it to appear in the contest), your contact number (which will be kept confidential), and an attachment of your work in .jpg, .jpeg, or .png formats with the caption you want to have for it written in the content of the e-mail.

  2. The topics for the content include Quarantine life, Online classes, Festivals in these times, College/Hostel life, Job placements, etc. Each entry should have a Pravega logo included at the top (click here to download). The dimensions of the entry are upto the participant.

  3. The entries will be judged by the panel based on its quality, originality, creativity and of course, its wittiness. The judging panel reserves the right to not award the prizes if the images do not meet the requirements stated and do not adhere to the Terms and Conditions (please read carefully) of this Contest.

  4. The results of the judging process are final and unamenable to change.


Awards

  1. Three winners will be declared. The prize money distribution will be as follows:
    1st prize: 600 INR
    2nd prize: 350 INR
    3rd prize: 250 INR

  2. Apart from these awards, the top 5 will receive shoutouts.

So, get ready to blow the brains out of peoples’ heads with your memes and follow us on our Instagram page to see your meme over there!


Eligibility: Open to All!
