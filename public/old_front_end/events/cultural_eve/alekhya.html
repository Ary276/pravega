Alekhya


Vision is an incredible power that creates defining memories and lasting experiences. Sight is an exquisite gift that both clears and clouds the truths and perspectives of life.

Caught up in a pandemic, we seldom take the time to have a look at contemporary lifestyle, the way art that captures its iconic moments and what it means to us. Art has always been a means to reflect on and appreciate the beauty and diversity of perspective in our lives. Alekhya presents the perfect opportunity to celebrate the role of interpretation in the wondrous visual experience!

You get to put your own unique spin on iconic pictures by eminent photographers using any artistic means and style of your choice*. From ancient figurative art to renaissance art to modern art techniques, set your creative spirit free and show us the story of a photograph as seen through your eyes. Win exciting prizes and share your art with people all over India!
