Proscenium and Footprints presents,

Relay Reels


Yes, we’re serious. This is indeed a Play competition, under the COVID-19 restrictions. Do not fret, we are not forcing you to break any social distancing guideline, nor are we preventing you from having the best Pravega experience that you had hoped for. We, actually, have something far better in the box.

So, form a group of as many talented actors you can and choose a theme of your choice to present it as a skit. Here is the catch, you don’t enact it together at a single place. Instead, break down the play into small scenes, that involve individual characters enacting them at their own home. Finally merge the scenes, as if it’s a one-shot piece (Does the movie 1917 ring any bells?). Well, just kidding, it doesn’t have to be that good. We recommend the shots to be in monochrome to ensure better continuity.

For ideas, view this video.



Rules and eligibility

  1. Participants are supposed to enact a skit/play corresponding to any chosen theme of their own in 8-10 min.
  2. Participants must form a group of 5 – 8 members
  3. To adhere to COVID-19 guidelines, each member of the group is expected to shoot his part of the video at his home, and then all the parts should be merged to get the final Relay Reel. To ensure continuity, the video should be in black & white.
  4. Send your video to pravegaproscenium@gmail.com, The videos will be posted in our Proscenium and Footprints channel in YouTube.


Awards

  1. Winners will be chosen on the basis of number of likes and views on our YouTube channel as well as by the panel of judges of Pravega. YouTube likes will constitute 30% weightage and the rest of the weightage lies on the decision of the judging panel.
  2. Three winners will be declared: The prize money distribution would be as follows: First – 15000 INR + Pravega E-Certificates + Shout-outs Second – 10000 INR + Pravega E-Certificates + Shout-outs Third – 5000 INR + Pravega E-Certificates + Shout-outs
  3. The winners in above three categories will be chosen solely by the panel of judges of Pravega. The winners will receive shoutouts and E-certificates.
