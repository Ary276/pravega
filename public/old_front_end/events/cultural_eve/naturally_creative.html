Naturally Creative

Time to put on your creative hats!

Pravega 2021 brings to you NATURALLY CREATIVE, a design workshop on biomimicry. We will take you through this fascinating process to generate interesting forms , starting from just a simple observation into the natural world around us. You will use tools like sketching, modeling and leave the workshop with a heightened design sensitivity and appreciation for Natureâ€™s design of things.

The best part is, there are no prerequisites to attend this workshop! This is for anyone wanting to get their feet wet in design and get their creative juices flowing.

Date and Time

Mark your calendars for 19th September OR 20th September (you can choose your day).
Timing - 11am to 4 pm (including a lunch break from 1-2pm) on both days.

Required Items

Mandatory items: Pencils, paper, and a good phone/digital camera.
Optional items: Clay or atta, cardboard, Paint 3D software.

Perks

E certificates to all participants, display of shortlisted works on Pravega's website and social media handle(s)

Eligibility

Open to all!
