Whodunnit

Whodunnit is an online mystery-solving game that is being conducted by Pravega'21. Groups of students would be trying to solve a crime with the help of online clues in the form of pdfs, text files, voice and video recordings, online interrogation etc. This will be a game that will challenge your deductive reasoning, interrogation and observation skills. It looks forward to bringing together teams of budding investigators and to give them a taste of crime-solving in a digital age.



Date and Time

30th July 9:00 AM to 1st August 9:00 AM



Limit on number of teams

There is an upper limit of 30 groups that will be participating in the game. The groups will be allowed to register and will be allocated a unique group number on a first come , first serve basis.



Eligibility

Any student from any university/school below the age of 27 is welcome to participate.
