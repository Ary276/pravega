BioBlitz


BioBlitz: a competition curated to cause intellectual shock and intense engrossment in the participants through the use of superior questioning designed to probe both core and frontier areas of Biology.

Whether you are a biology veteran, a biology enthusiast, a jack-of-all-trades or just curious to explore the interdisciplinary nature of biology, this competition will present challenges and learning opportunities for all. It is a competition that will challenge not only your knowledge but also your perspective of Biology. In short, it is a competition like no other.

The game is simple: solve questions and prove your calibre. Those who shall pass the prelims shall face a more enigmatic trial: working through challenging problems and defending their analysis not only in front of a panel of subject experts but also other participants.

Do you dare to step into the Blitz?
