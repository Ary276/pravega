Chemenigma presents,

Elementary


Elementary aims to probe creativity and critical thinking in students and enthusiasts in Chemistry. There are two segments of the event:

  1. Elementary Lecture Series ↓
  2. Elementary Quiz ↓

This event started on August, 2020 and is planned to continue till December, 2020.

Elementary Lecture Series


Do you want to explore the wide spectrum of Chemistry and learn about its different fields and witness their grandeur? We've got your back! Elementary brings to you a series of webinars where participants get to learn about new and upcoming fields of research in Chemistry from eminent chemists around the world! So come on and watch the experts speak and leave with the motivation to dig deeper.

Open to everyone!

Elementary Quiz


This segment involves thought-provoking questions related to classical as well as evolving fields in the extensive discipline of Chemistry every week. Participate and answer multiple questions posted by us each week (days to be updated in advance) and compete to rake points and maintain your position on the leader-board! The event is planned to span four weeks, so make sure you hold your position at the top!
