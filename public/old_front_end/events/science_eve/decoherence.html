Spooky quizzes

Spooky quizzes is an online physics based competition, where an individual tests his analytical, thinking, mathematical, logical reasoning and deduction skills. Gear up and get ready to analyze physical problems in unique and interesting ways. It enables you develop the excitement to the field and test the real physicist in you. Register to have fun solving problems in original ways!!

Quizzes will be conducted online in three rounds - Theoretical and Experiment Analysis Rounds (Objective - MCQ) and a Final Round (Subjective). Top five performers (based on both the quizzes) shall appear for a subjective round, where participants have to find solution(s) to the given question(s). This will be done once in every 3 weeks.

Eligibility: Interested High school students(11th and 12th class) and Undergraduates(1st,2nd,3rd year) register through the link below.

Prizes A common leaderboard will be maintained for all the participants for each cycle. Top two performers in the subjective round will be awarded cash prize. Topper will get Rs 1000 and runner up will get Rs 500. All the top five participants appearing for subjective round will also receive e-certificates.
