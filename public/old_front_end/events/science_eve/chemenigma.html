Chemenigma


A competition that tests your calibre in Chemistry through thought-provoking questions that demand knowledge coupled with creativity. Enjoy yourselves and compete with students across the country while displaying your skills and ingenuity in solving problems.

Prove that you are among the best in the Prelims to qualify for the Finals!

The Final event will truly bring out the Enigma of Chemenigma where the finalists would have to work their way through challenging problems which may or may not have known solutions with the exciting opportunity to present and defend their work in front of leading Chemists in the panel of judges!



Dates

  • Prelims: 2:00 PM - 6:00 PM IST, 29th June, 2021
  • Finals: TBD. During Pravega '21 (July 29 - August 1, 2021)


Eligibility

Students in class 11 or above (till 2nd year UG) and have not started their 3rd year of UG are eligible. Teams may have at most two participants. Individual participation is allowed.



Prizes

Cash Prizes over 30K for winners and certificates for all finalists.



Rules and Regulations

  • Prelims:
    • 8 - 10 Objective questions and 5 - 6 subjective questions
    • Duration: 4 hours including submission time
    • No late submission will be entertained
    • 6 best teams will be selected for the Finals.
  • Finals: Details will be updated soon.
